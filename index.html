Discovery Questions I Ask in Pre-Sales Calls

These are the questions I use to qualify, uncover pain, and position the right solution — not a script, but a framework I've built through real technical conversations.

🔍

Qualification

  • "What's driving this conversation — a specific incident, a cost review, or a strategic initiative?"
  • "Who owns the infrastructure decision — engineering, finance, or the CTO?"
  • "What does success look like in 90 days?"
⚡

Pain Uncovering

  • "How often do you have unplanned downtime, and what does an hour of outage cost you?"
  • "What's your current deployment frequency — and how long does a rollback take?"
  • "Are your engineers spending more time keeping the lights on than shipping features?"
💡

Technical Fit

  • "Are your workloads bursty or steady-state? That changes the architecture significantly."
  • "Do you have compliance requirements — SOC 2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA? That affects the design from day one."
  • "What's your team's Kubernetes experience? I want to propose what they can actually operate."
📊

Value & ROI

  • "If we could cut your AWS bill 20-30% while improving availability — what would that free up for the business?"
  • "Have you run a TCO analysis on serverless vs. your current EC2 setup?"
  • "What would it mean for your team to deploy on every PR merge with zero manual steps?"
